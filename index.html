WHY DESIGNING?
An Inside Story

I still remember, when i got my first mobile phone (my reward for achieving 85% in Class X), i used to download several apps from play store just beacuse i found their icons attractive. Any application that had an attractive icon, became the member of my mobile app list.

Bit by bit, my interest increased and then i started playing with the themes or colour schemes of mobile phones. I used to create custom themes with my own palette of colours.

In class XI, i got a chance to work with HTML and CSS. This was big exposure for me as i started designing web pages, implementing themes of my taste and flavor. Drop by drop my comprehension increased and thoughts of new designs started originating.

After working for two years with HTML, CSS and basic Javascript, i started finding difficulties in implementing complex desings and was feeling limited. I was able to visualize designs in my thought process but was not able to fetch it out or express it out on screen. So i did a little research and decided to learn Adobe Photoshop.

After learning this new tool, i started prototyping User Interfaces and other graphic elements. This led my interest to increase in fields of UI and Graphic Designing. I had portrayed several prototypes and designs on Behance platform from Adobe.

Now i am working parallelly with designing and development of Web Applications. Learning new technologies and increasing my knowledge stack.
